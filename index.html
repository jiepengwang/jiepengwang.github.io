Jiepeng Wang

Ph.D. Candidate, HKU

jiepeng@connect.hku.hk

I'm a final-year PhD student at The University of Hong Kong (HKU), advised by Prof. Wenping Wang and Prof. Taku Komura. Before that, I got my master's degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Genliang Chen and Prof. Hao Wang, and got my bachelor's degree at Shandong University. I was also a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia from June 2022 to December 2023, closely working with Dr. Hao Pan, Dr. Yang Liu, and Dr. Xin Tong. My research interests include 3D reconstruction and generation, neural rendering, and robotics.
