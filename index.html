About Me

I'm a researcher specializing in generative AI at Institute of Artificial Intelligence (TeleAI), China Telecom.

I received my Ph.D. at The University of Hong Kong (HKU), advised by Prof. Wenping Wang and Prof. Taku Komura. Before that, I got my master's degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Genliang Chen and Prof. Hao Wang, and got my bachelor's degree at Shandong University.

I was also a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia from June 2022 to December 2023, closely working with Dr. Hao Pan, Dr. Yang Liu, and Dr. Xin Tong.

My research interests lie in generative visual models (particularly video diffusion models), world models, and robotics. I aim to integrate these areas to enable more comprehensive perception, prediction, and real-world interaction, ultimately advancing the development of system-level intelligence.

I'm looking for highly motivated research interns to work on video generation and world models. If you are interested in these areas or would like to discuss any of these research topics, feel free to send me an email (please include your CV if applying for an internship).
